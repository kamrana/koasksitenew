Tracking the progresion of knee osteoarthritis just got easier!

KOASK provides you a platform from which you can manage and track the progression of your osteoarthritis. Most importantly it provides an ease of mind.

Estimate your severity

The ability to see the current progression of your knee osteoarthritis

Tracks 3 Variables

A focus on pain, functionality, and stiffness.

Customized Exercise plan

See recommended excersies for your severity level. Keeps you on-track and motivated!

Easy of use

Developed with functionality in mind. The application is simple to use.

7-Year prediction

Provides you with a 7 year severity estimate, so you can be better informed.

Be one step ahead

Tracking the progression allows you to make better decisions and keeps your osteoarthritis in check.
